Position matters - mount your phone behind the rear-view mirror for the clearest forward view and minimal obstruction.

Keep it charged - plug your phone into your car charger so PixlCam can record the whole journey without draining your battery.

Protect important clips - if something happens, tap the protect button immediately so the footage isn't overwritten on the next loop.
