Frequently Asked Questions

Is PixlCam available on iPhone?

What is PixlCam?

PixlCam is a free dash cam app that turns your smartphone into a dashcam. It automatically overwrites clips to prevent your phone from getting full, and protects important footage so you always have proof if something happens on the road.

Is PixlCam free?

Yes - PixlCam is completely free to install and use.

When will PixlCam launch on the App Store?

PixlCam for iPhone is currently in active development. Join the waitlist and we'll email you the moment it's available on the Apple App Store - you won't have to keep checking.

Does PixlCam require any extra hardware?

PixlCam uses the camera already built into your smartphone. You will need a windscreen mount to position your phone, then just download the app and start driving protected.

Why do I need a dashcam app?

In the event of an accident or incident on the road, footage is often the difference between proving what happened and being blamed for something that wasn't your fault. Most drivers have no dashcam at all - PixlCam fixes that for free, using the phone already in your pocket.
